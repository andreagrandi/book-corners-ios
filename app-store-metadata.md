## Description (max 4000 characters)
Book Corners helps you discover, share, and contribute to community book exchanges in your neighborhood and around the world.

Browse a growing directory of community book exchanges near you or anywhere on the map. Each library listing includes its location, photo, address, and details like wheelchair accessibility and whether it's lit at night.

Features:

- Nearby libraries: See community book exchanges close to your current location, sorted by distance
- Interactive map: Explore libraries on a full-screen Apple Maps view with pins and filters
- Search and filter: Find libraries by city, country, postal code, or keywords
- Detailed listings: View photos, addresses, metadata, and get walking or driving directions
- Submit a library: Found a community book exchange that's not listed? Add it with a photo and location
- Submit a photo: Help keep listings up to date by submitting a new photo for any library
- Report issues: Let the community know if a library is damaged, missing, or has incorrect information
- Directions: Get walking directions via Apple Maps or Google Maps

Book Corners is a community-driven project. All submissions are reviewed before being published. Create a free account to start contributing.

Whether you're a book lover looking for your next read, a traveler exploring new neighborhoods, or someone who wants to support community sharing, Book Corners makes it easy to find and support community book exchanges everywhere.

# Privacy Labels

When filling out the App Privacy section in App Store Connect, select the following:

## Data Collected

### 1. Location — Precise Location
- Purpose: **App Functionality**
- Linked to user: No
- Used to track: No
- Notes: Used to show nearby libraries and center the map. Not stored on server for logged-out users. Submitted with new library listings (user-provided, not background tracking).

### 2. Contact Info — Email Address
- Purpose: **App Functionality**
- Linked to user: Yes
- Used to track: No
- Notes: Collected during account registration. Used for account management only.

### 3. Contact Info — Name (Username)
- Purpose: **App Functionality**
- Linked to user: Yes
- Used to track: No
- Notes: Username chosen during registration. Displayed in account profile.

### 4. Photos or Videos — Photos
- Purpose: **App Functionality**
- Linked to user: Yes
- Used to track: No
- Notes: Users can select photos from their library when submitting a new library listing, updating a library photo, or reporting an issue. Photos are uploaded to the server.

### 5. Identifiers — User ID
- Purpose: **App Functionality**
- Linked to user: Yes
- Used to track: No
- Notes: Internal user ID assigned at registration. Used to associate submissions with accounts.

## Data NOT Collected
- Health & Fitness
- Financial Info
- Sensitive Info
- Browsing History
- Search History (searches are not logged server-side)
- Contacts
- Diagnostics
- Usage Data
- Purchases
